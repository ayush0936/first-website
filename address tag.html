Understanding the <address> Tag

The <address> tag is used to provide contact information for the author or owner of a document or an article. It can contain physical addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, and other contact details.

Tip from Ayush

When using the <address> tag, make sure to include relevant contact information. This helps users reach out to you easily.
